Design Notes

  • Landscape: All 5 dancers visible at once in columns. Judge can scan across everyone simultaneously.
  • Portrait (1 Column): Scrollable vertical list showing 2-3 tiles at a time. Maintains same interaction pattern (tap to score).
  • Portrait (2 Columns): Side-by-side tiles with abbreviated labels ("Tech" instead of "Technique"). Fits 4 tiles on screen, less scrolling needed. More compressed but sees more dancers at once.
  • Color coding: Green border = fully scored (5/5), Orange = partial (3/5), Gray = not started (0/5)
  • Button size: Score buttons are sized for comfortable touch targets (~44px minimum tap area in 1-column, ~36px in 2-column)
  • Completion indicator: Shows progress at a glance (e.g., "3/5" means 3 of 5 categories scored)
  • Consistent layout: Each dancer tile uses identical structure regardless of orientation
